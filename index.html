Intro

How's it going? If you're here, you probably already know who I am. But for those who don't, nice to meet you! I'm Jayden and I'm an ACE certified strength and nutrition coach. I'm a geologist by trade, but my passion for fitness and movement took over and I just couldn't work with rocks anymore. Currently my training revolves around olympic weightlifting, but previously I've trained in bodybuilding and powerlifting. If you wanna connect more with me, feel free to chedck out my Instagram @jaydotse :')

My goal as a coach is to help you become the best version of yourself. That means having the sick physique you've always wanted, enjoying training, eating like a king, being a functional, mobile human being, and learning all the why's behind all the do's so you can make educated choices for yourself. If you're interested in working with me, send me an e-mail and we'll set up a consultation call and see if we're a good fit for each other! Look forward to hearing from you soon!

About

You are an individual different from everyone else, so why does it make sense for you to be handed a cookie cutter program? All your individual needs are taken into account when designing your program. If you can promise me commitment, I can promise you results. My training revovlves heavily around functionality and movement. I want you to build muscle, burn fat, and be pain-free. It makes me so angry when a coach responds to their client's pains by avoiding the exercise the causes it. That's like putting a bandage on a car accident wound - it doesn't deal with the root of the issue. This is the reason why I emphasize movement.

Thank you for your interest in my services and I apologize in advance if I'm unable to work with you. My coaching spots are limited so I can ensure quality service to all of my clients. When new spots open up, I will be sure to update this on my platforms!
