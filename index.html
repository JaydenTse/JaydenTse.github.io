How's it going? If you're here, you probably already know who I am. But for those who don't, nice to meet you!

INTRO

I'm Jayden and I'm an ACE certified strength and nutrition coach. I'm a geologist by trade, but my passion for fitness and movement took over and I just couldn't work with rocks anymore. Currently my training revolves around olympic weightlifting, but previously I've trained in bodybuilding and powerlifting. If you wanna connect more with me, feel free to chedck out my Instagram @jaydotse :')

MY GOALS

My goal as a coach is to help you become the best version of yourself. That means having the sick physique you've always wanted, enjoying training, eating like a king, being a functional, mobile human being, and learning all the why's behind all the do's so you can make educated choices for yourself. If you're interested in working with me, send me an e-mail and we'll set up a consultation call and see if we're a good fit for each other! Look forward to hearing from you soon!
