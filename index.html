MY GOAL

My goal as a coach is to help you become the best version of yourself. That means having the sick physique you've always wanted, enjoying training, eating like a king, being a functional, mobile human being, and learning all the why's behind all the do's so you can make educated choices for yourself. I see too many "coaches" with no credentials offering subpar service, so I want to make sure I hold you accountable to all your goals. If you're interested in working with me, send me an e-mail and we'll set up a consultation call and see if we're a good fit for each other! Look forward to hearing from you soon!

My Story

If you're familiar with Asian culture, you'll know that being anything apart from skinny is highly frowned upon. I tried to stay within this skinny box for around 18 years and had a lot of side eyes cast at me when I started to pack on muscle. It was after my first terrible break up that I started to invest in myself and commmit to the gym. Within a relationship where I had no control, it was in fitness that I found myself again. Needless to say I wouldn't give this up for anything! 7 years later, I can say it was the best decision I've ever made. Now I want to change as many lives as possible and help others achieve their potential.
